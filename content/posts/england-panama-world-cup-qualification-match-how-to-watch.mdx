## What happened
England is set to compete for a place in the Round of 16 against an already eliminated Panamanian squad. The match is scheduled for this Saturday at the New York/New Jersey Stadium, kicking off at 5 p.m. ET / 4 p.m. CT. This encounter follows Panama's challenging game against Croatia in Toronto, where they were unable to find the back of the net.

For viewers in the United States, the game will be broadcast live on Telemundo's over-the-air signal. Additionally, subscribers to Peacock Premium or Premium Plus can stream the match live. Coinciding with this game, Croatia and Ghana will be playing their respective match in Philadelphia, which can be viewed on Universo, Telexitos, and Peacock, as Telemundo continues its comprehensive coverage of the FIFA World Cup, broadcasting the majority of the 104 matches in Spanish.
