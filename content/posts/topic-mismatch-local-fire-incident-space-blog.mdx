## How to think about it
When evaluating topics for a specialized blog, it's crucial to filter potential subjects through the lens of the target audience and the blog's niche. For a space and astronomy blog, every topic should ideally offer a pathway to discuss celestial bodies, space missions, scientific discoveries, or the broader implications of humanity's quest to understand the cosmos. If a topic cannot be genuinely connected to these themes without significant fabrication or a strained analogy, it is likely unsuitable. Prioritizing thematic consistency ensures that the content remains valuable and relevant to the dedicated readership.

<Callout type="warning">Attempting to force unrelated local news into a specialized science blog can dilute brand identity and disengage the target audience. Always prioritize thematic relevance.</Callout>
